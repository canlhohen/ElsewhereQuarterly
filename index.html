Can't afford a subscription?
You can still read.

We don't ask for proof of anything. If the standard price is genuinely a barrier for you — you're a student, you're based in a low-income country, you're going through a difficult period — apply for free access. We will say yes.

We also offer geographic pricing. Readers in many lower-income countries are charged automatically at a rate that reflects local purchasing power. If you are not sure whether this applies to you, just email us.

Common questions
When does my subscription start?
Immediately. You get access to the full digital archive the moment your payment is confirmed.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from your account dashboard. Annual subscriptions are non-refundable but you keep access until the end of the year.
Where do you ship print copies?
Currently within the EU. We are working on expanding to the UK, US, and further. If you are outside the EU and want a print subscription, email us and we will work something out.
What is the Solidarity rate?
€2/month for full digital access. No verification required. We trust you. Every Supporter subscription funds two Solidarity subscriptions — if you choose to pay more, you are directly subsidising access for someone who cannot.
How do I set up an institutional subscription?
Email us at editor@elsewherequarterly.com. We will send you an invoice and set up access for your library, department, or organisation.
Is my payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment platforms. We never see or store your card details.
